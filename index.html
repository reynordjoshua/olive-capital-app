About Olive Capital

Olive Capital is an independent financial analysis practice built around one idea: judgment backed by numbers, not numbers instead of judgment. We build the models and tools we use ourselves — this Autonomous Financial Analyst is one of them, developed in-house to turn raw financial statements into a ratio read, a valuation, and a written verdict in minutes rather than hours.

What we work on

Financial statement analysis, valuation modeling (DCF & scenario-based), budgeting and forecasting, KPI reporting, and building the automation tools that make analyst work faster and more consistent.

Vision

A world where rigorous financial analysis isn't gated behind large teams and expensive terminals — where a single well-built model can do the work of a desk.

Mission

To build practical, transparent financial tools — grounded in real accounting and valuation methodology — that help people read a company's numbers clearly and make better decisions faster.
